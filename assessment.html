SuperBowl LII Eagles vs. Patriots:
"When the underdogs became the top dogs!"


Philadelphia Eagles won SuperBowl LII in 2018 against the New England Patriots with a score of 41-33. As a die-hard Eagles fan, I have been waiting for this moment, my whole life! I was born during an Eagles vs. Cowboys game. My Dad even asked my Mom to wait until half-time to deliver me because the Eagles were down. I was then born at half-time and the Eagles ended up winning 20-10! From that point on the Eagles were my team. I suffered many losses with them and celebrated wins. But the best win was winning the SuperBowl in 2018!

Quote:"We were a bunch of underdogs. And you know what an underdog is? It's a hungry dog!" -- Jason Kelce, Philadelphia Eagles, winner of SuperBowl LII

New England Patriots QB: Tom Brady, will always be one of the greatest QB's of all time! They have now become my second team, thanks to my Husband! He grew up watching the Patriots living in Boston.It was an awesome game to see the Eagles carry out the biggest win in history for the Eagles against the Patriots during the SuperBowl LII.

Quote:"I was the kid that the 199th pick that never had the body for it. People didn't think I'd play one year in the NFL, and now I'm going on my 17th year." -- Tom Brady,QB for the New England Patriots, 2000-2019
